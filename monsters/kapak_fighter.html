Kapak Draconian Fighter

medium dragon (draconian), chaotic evil

Armor Class

13

Hit Points

52

Speed

30ft

Proficiencies

(+3 proficiency bonus)

Saving Throws

Con (+5), Dex (+5)

Skills

Perception +2, Stealth +5

Senses

darkvision 120ft, passive Perception 12

Languages

Common, Draconic

Challange

3 (700 XP)

Glide

The kapak can use its wings to glide, negating any damage from a fall of any height. It can also travel horizontally up to four times the vertical distance it descends.

Death Throes

When the kapak dies, its body instantly dissolves into a 5-foot radius pool of acid. All creatures within this area suffer 8 (2d8) points of acid damage each round they remain in the pool. The acid evaporates in 1 minute. All armor, weapons, an items carried by the kapak suffer damage from the acid as well.

Poison

The kapak’s poison doesn’t affect other kapaks.

Sneak Attack (1/turn)

The kapak deals an extra 14 (4d6) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of the kapak that isn’t incapacitated and the kapak doesn’t have disadvantage on the roll.

Actions

Multiattack

Kapak Fighter makes two melee or ranged attacks with weapon.

Shortsword

Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 1) piercing damage and the target must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw, becoming poisoned and paralyzed on a failed save for 10 minutes.

Bite

Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 1) piercing damage and the target must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw, becoming poisoned and paralyzed on a failed save for 10 minutes.

Claws

Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) slashing damage.

Shortbow

Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
