Leadership

For 1 minute, Derek can utter a special command or warning whenever a nonhostile creature that it can see within 30 feet of it makes an attack roll or a saving throw. The creature can add a d4 to its roll provided it can hear and understand Derek. A creature can benefit from only one Leadership die at a time. This effect ends if Derek is incapacitated.
