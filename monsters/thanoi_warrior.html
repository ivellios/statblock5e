Actions

Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6 + 5) piercing damage.

Harpoon

Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d6 + 6) piercing damage. If the target is a Huge or smaller creature, it must succeed on a Strength contest against the thanoi or be pulled up to 20 feet toward the thanoi.

Bonus actions

Sliding Charge

When Thanoi warrior moves at least 20 ft, he can attack with this attack as a bonus action. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d10 + 6) piercing damage. On hit target makes Constitution saving throw and is prone on failure. Recharge 5-6.
