Arcane Recovery

Once per day when you finish a Short Rest, you can choose expended Spell Slots to recover. The Spell Slots can have a combined level that is equal to or less than half your wizard level (rounded up), and none of the slots can be 6th level or higher.

Arcane Tradition: School of Transmutation

Transmutation Savant

Beginning when you select this school at 2nd level, the gold and time you must spend to copy a transmutation spell into your spellbook is halved.

Minor Alchemy

You can temporarily alter the physical properties o f one nonmagical object, changing it from one substance into another. You can transform wood, stone (but not a gemstone), iron, copper, or silver. 10 minutes/1 cubic foot. Duration 1h or concentration.

Transmuter’s Stone

Starting at 6th level, you can spend 8 hours creating a transmuter’s stone that stores transmutation magic.

Staff of Magius

The staff of Magius can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff that grants a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls. While you hold it, it provides a +2 bonus to armor class. The staff has 12 charges for the following properties. It regains 1d4+4 expended charges at night in the light of Solinari. The staff doubles spell durations, and adds 1 damage point to all die rolls for each spell.

Secrets

The Staff has hidden powers. DM should roll a d20. On a 18-20, a random effect occurs, rolled from the list below on 1d12. Attempt a DC 15 Intelligence (Arcana) check to master the effect.

1) Daylight (3 charges), 2) Darkness (2 charges), 3) Detect Magic (1 charge), 6) Magic Missile (1 charge), 8) Levitate (2 charges), 10) Dispel Magic (3 charges), 11) Invisibility (2 charges)
