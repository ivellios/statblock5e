Staff of Magius

The staff of Magius can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff that grants a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls. While you hold it, it provides a +2 bonus to armor class. The staff has 12 charges for the following properties. It regains 1d4+4 expended charges at night in the light of Solinari. The staff doubles spell durations, and adds 1 damage point to all die rolls for each spell.
